# SaaS GTM Risk Patterns

## ICP & Market Risks
- **Risk: ICP too broad** — Messaging resonates with no one because it's written for everyone. Early Warning: Low email reply rates (< 2%), generic objections on demos ("not sure it's for us"). Mitigation: Narrow to one job title at one company stage in one industry. Owner: Founder / Head of Marketing.
- **Risk: Wrong ICP assumed** — Product built for one persona, but actual buyers are a different role. Early Warning: Demo attendees are not the assumed persona. Mitigation: Run 10 discovery calls before finalizing ICP; ask "who signed the last SaaS tool purchase?" Owner: Founder.
- **Risk: Market too small** — TAM insufficient for VC-scale growth. Early Warning: Exhausted outreach list after 200 contacts. Mitigation: Validate TAM with bottom-up calculation (accounts × ACV). Owner: Founder / CFO.
- **Risk: Seasonal demand** — B2B SaaS buying slows in August and December. Early Warning: Open rates drop > 20% in these months. Mitigation: Front-load pipeline building in Q1 and Q3. Owner: Sales Lead.

## Product-Market Fit Risks
- **Risk: Value unclear in demo** — Prospects attend demos but don't convert to trials. Early Warning: Demo-to-trial rate < 20%. Mitigation: Restructure demo to show the "aha moment" within first 5 minutes; use customer's own data. Owner: Founder / Sales.
- **Risk: High activation drop-off** — Users sign up but never complete setup. Early Warning: < 30% complete onboarding within 7 days. Mitigation: Add white-glove onboarding for first 20 customers; simplify setup to < 3 steps. Owner: Head of Product.
- **Risk: Feature-request mismatch** — Users request features that indicate wrong use case. Early Warning: > 50% of feature requests are outside core use case. Mitigation: Conduct weekly user interviews; add a feedback tagging system. Owner: Product Lead.

## Competitive Risks
- **Risk: Incumbent adds competing feature** — Zendesk, HubSpot, or Intercom ships a native version of your core feature. Early Warning: Competitor release notes or TechCrunch articles mentioning your feature area. Mitigation: Accelerate differentiation roadmap; focus on depth over breadth. Owner: CEO / Product Lead.
- **Risk: New well-funded competitor** — A VC-backed competitor enters with 10× your marketing budget. Early Warning: Crunchbase funding alert for keywords in your space. Mitigation: Lock in early customers with annual contracts; focus on a niche the competitor won't serve. Owner: CEO.
- **Risk: Price war** — Competitor undercuts pricing significantly. Early Warning: Multiple prospects mention competitor pricing in objections. Mitigation: Reframe on ROI (cost savings / time saved), not price; add value-based pricing tier. Owner: CEO / Head of Sales.

## Execution Risks
- **Risk: Founder bandwidth** — GTM slows because founder is still building the product. Early Warning: < 5 outreach messages sent per week. Mitigation: Time-box GTM to 2–3 hours/day; hire part-time SDR or use automation (Apollo sequences). Owner: Founder.
- **Risk: Content treadmill** — Content channel requires more output than team can sustain. Early Warning: Publishing cadence drops below once per week after Month 1. Mitigation: Batch-create 4 weeks of content in one session; repurpose long-form to short-form. Owner: Marketing Lead.
- **Risk: Channel not working** — Primary channel produces < 50% of targeted leads after 4 weeks. Early Warning: Week 4 pipeline less than 25% of 8-week target. Mitigation: Pivot to backup channel (defined in advance); run a 2-week sprint on the new channel. Owner: Head of GTM.
- **Risk: Low webinar attendance** — Webinar registrations significantly below target with < 2 weeks to go. Early Warning: Registrations < 50% of target at T-14 days. Mitigation: Switch to 1:1 recorded demo outreach; Sales Director initiates direct outreach to registered leads. Owner: Sales Director.

## Financial Risks
- **Risk: CAC too high** — Cost to acquire each customer makes the unit economics unsustainable. Early Warning: CAC payback period > 18 months after first 5 customers. Mitigation: Shift from outbound to product-led or community channels; reduce sales cycle length. Owner: CFO / Founder.
- **Risk: Free trial abuse** — Users churn after trial without converting, consuming support resources. Early Warning: Trial-to-paid conversion rate < 10% after 6 weeks. Mitigation: Add trial qualification criteria (company size, use case fit); add a credit card requirement or limit trial scope. Owner: Head of Product / Sales.
- **Risk: Budget exhausted before traction** — GTM spend runs out before hitting a repeatable acquisition channel. Early Warning: Runway < 6 months with no paying customers. Mitigation: Cut paid channels immediately; focus exclusively on zero-cost channels (community, outbound). Owner: CFO / Founder.
